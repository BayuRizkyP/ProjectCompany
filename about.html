About Us

Dimcargo We established on 2020 As Professional International Freight Forwarder (N.V.O.C.C) company in the freighting business, our main objective is providing the most dedicated quality service and economical freight rates that is highly demanded by world traders and shippers today. DimCargo provides professional consultation and advice to customer in the best possible way to ship their valuable cargoes by containerised vessels, LCL Consolidation and Air freight world wide.

Our Goal And Our Commitment

Our goal is to provide our valued customers the assurance of ease of operation and cost competitiveness for their business needs. We look forward to the opportunity to demonstrate our commitment to customer satisfaction and service quality. And Our Teams passion for learning and growth energizes our goal for service effectiveness and success. We thrive for quality service and commitment. We continuously expand relationships and form partnerships. Building a strong working relationship with our clientele enables us to understand and meet their strategic business requirements and demands. We strongly believe that you will find in us the right services and solutions for your logistics needs.

Vision DimCargo

Build up customer relationships with integrity and ethics to achieve our goals and remain competitive in the global market.
