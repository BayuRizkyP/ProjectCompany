Our ServiceS

As freight forwarding specialists DimCargo are perfectly placed to assist with your freight transportation requirements.

Special Service

We provide a range of services, including project handling, inland transportation, office and household removals, as well as reliable subcontractors for special equipment and facilities.

Sea Freight

DimCargo provides full ocean freight service to move your commercial and project cargo all over the city. We leverage our long-standing relationships with our carrier partners to provide you with efficient and economical solutions for all your shipping needs.

AIR FREIGHT

Over our wide network of air routes, DimCargo can deliver your cargo anywhere in the city in a matter of hours. We offer direct service from most major Indonesia airports, ensuring the safe delivery of your air freight on time, every time.
